IDEATION

Ideation is a collaborative creative method in which designers produce ideas in sessions. Ideation is the process of creating a large number of thoughts about a specific topic without making any attempt to judge or evaluate them. While the ultimate goal of ideation is to produce a high-quality design that solves a specific problem.
